POWER LINES

POWER LINE EXAMPLES

Overhead Power Line

These power lines are these towering poles that transmit electricity across large distances. This is a low cost method of power transmission

You'll notice that this one is made of metal. This likely means that it is a main or checkpoint source of electrical energy

Overhead Power Line (Lampost)

Some Lamposts, primarily in less-urbanised areas, act as power lines. They essentially are multi-taskers

The main safety issue with these, is that they can easily burn down and cause fires and generally are a hazard if toppled over. More information in the links below.

Overhead Line

Ever wonder how a train moves? That's what these are for. The Overhead Line is used to transmit electrical energy to transport such as trains, trams and the like.

You'll notice these a lot throughout your train rides, and are placed on the respective sides of the tracks.
